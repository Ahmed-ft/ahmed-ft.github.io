WHO ARE WE?
Baderaldeen Oil and Gas Services Company was established more than a decade ago (14 years ago) in the preparation Oil installations and heavy and light transport, specialized in providing all services to the fields Oil and gas and everything that includes activities related to oil and gas.

Its primary objective is to engage in engineering, construction and specialized manpower services. The company's strategy is based on expansion in the field of engineering, procurement, construction and operation And maintenance in all parts of Libya and abroad. As for the field of building and operating, we are pioneers in this specialization for the company and companies Domestic and global strategy aims the company on it.

Oil standards and services, industrial safety, quality and specialized expertise, we make sure that Specialized workers perform very professionally. We value the philosophy of initiating and completing projects on time on budget With the highest standards in safety and quality.
