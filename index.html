OUR STORY

To meet challenging goals by engaging the talent and passion of people who believe there is always a better way

Baderaldeen Oil and Gas Services Company was established more than a decade ago (14 years ago) in the preparation Oil installations and heavy and light transport, specialized in providing all services to the fields Oil and gas and everything that includes activities related to oil and gas.

Its primary objective is to engage in engineering, construction and specialized manpower services. The company's strategy is based on expansion in the field of engineering, procurement, construction and operation And maintenance in all parts of Libya and abroad.

Our goal is to exceed client expectation by offering outstanding service and to be the leading provider of professional services and deliver highest quality services at competitive prices.
